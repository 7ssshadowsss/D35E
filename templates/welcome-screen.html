Welcome to DND 3.5E SRD implementation for Foundry VTT! It was created from love for old DND editions and aims to provide full 3.5E SRD implementation and system flexible enough for anything else.

Happy GMing,
Rughalt

News

Release 0.84 brings Enhancement support and most of Magic Weapons and Shields from SRD fully featured. You can read more on how Enhancement system works on project Wiki (linked below).

Visit Enhancements page on Wiki

Release 0.84 adds new features to Attack dialog - ability to use Power Attack, Manyshot, assigning weapons for Two-Weapon Fighting and more directly from Attack window, with correct calculations happening in the background.

Did you know DND 3.5E comes with custom skin for Foundry? You - or any of your players - can enable it in options for themselves!
